OUR MISSION

More Than an App. A Movement.

We believe care is a right, not a privilege. KinCare was founded on the radical idea that every family — no matter their income level, race, or family structure — deserves access to safe, reliable care. We're not just building an app. We're building a community where neighbors lift each other up, where corporations step up, and where no parent has to choose between going to work and their child's safety.

PROGRAMS

Six Paths to Affordable Care

We meet families where they are — with flexible programs designed for every situation.

🎚️

Sliding Scale

Pay what you can — from $0 to full price. Our income-verified sliding scale ensures cost is never a barrier to care. You set the rate that works for your family.

🔄

Mutual Aid Matching

Swap care hours with other families at zero cost. You watch my kids Tuesday, I watch yours Thursday. Our app matches compatible families automatically.

⭐

Community Credits

Earn free care hours by volunteering in your community. Tutor a neighbor's child, help at a food bank, or mentor a teen — and earn credits toward care.

💛

Sponsor a Family

When you book care, you can add $2 to sponsor a family in need. 100% of sponsor contributions go directly to subsidizing care for qualifying families.

🏢

Corporate Grants

We partner with companies committed to social impact. Corporate sponsors fund care packages for underserved communities in their areas.

🏛️

Government Programs

We accept CCDF vouchers, state childcare subsidies, and partner with local agencies. If you qualify for public assistance, we make it seamless to use.
